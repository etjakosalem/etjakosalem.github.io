Scrambled Egg

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

Method
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and egg
  4. Using spatula, fold the eggs from the edge
    of the pan to center every 20
    seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the
    heat(it will continue to cook
    on the plate until served)
